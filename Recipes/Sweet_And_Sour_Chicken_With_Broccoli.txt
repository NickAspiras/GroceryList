Sweet And Sour Chicken With Broccoli

1 tbsp canola / avocado oil
1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 half cup orange juice
6 cups small broccoli flroets
3 tbsp rice vinegar
2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
1 half tbsp cornstarch
2 tbsp honey
1 tbsp finely chopped garlic
1 quarter teaspoon crushed red pepper
1 half tsp kosher salt
